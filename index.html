In particular, we take pride in delivering differentiated solutions to our partners. We have a diverse skillset and over 50 years of experience in helping brands create a strong presence across both digital and traditional media.


Traditional banking is in a state of unprecedented flux. Under fire from FinTechs, big banks are spending billions of dollars on digital solutions, wooing back audiences that, post 2008, have been highly skeptical and cynical about their motives. Smaller, regional financial institutions stand to lose out significantly. We’re here to help guide you through the ever-changing landscape, whether you’re a financial brand, banking institution or financial technology startup.

Our founding client, Meed, is a uniquely constructed financial program and corporate ecosystem. Meed and its Member Banks have completely re-thought personal finance in light of today's sharing economy and are poised to shake up the entire digital financial services market.

6th Street is responsible for delivering all digital and print content for Meed, from website design and content creation to social media community management and print designs for in-branch collateral.
