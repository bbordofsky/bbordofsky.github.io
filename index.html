At 6th Street Mktg we focus upon providing marketing strategy
and executional solutions

In particular, we take pride in delivering exceptional results to financial brands, banking insitutions, and financial technology startups. We have a diverse skillset and over 50 years of experience in helping brands create a strong presence across digital and traditional mediums.

Our founding client, Meed, is a uniquely constructed financial app and ecosystem. Meed and its Member Banks are poised to shake up the entire digital financial services market.

6th Street is responsible for delivering all online content for Meed, from website design and content creation to social media community management and print designs for in-branch collateral.
